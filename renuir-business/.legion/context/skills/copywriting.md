# Skill: Copywriting
## When to use
Use this skill when writing or refining UX copy, landing page content, or developer-facing docs.

## Steps (numbered)
1. Identify the target audience: individuals, businesses, or developers.
2. For individuals, write with a warm and human tone that reduces stress and builds trust.
3. For businesses, write with a professional, efficiency-focused tone centered on operations outcomes.
4. For developers, write with clear and direct language, concrete examples, and minimal ambiguity.
5. Keep claims specific, concise, and aligned with current product capabilities.
6. Review final copy for clarity, empathy, and audience fit.

## Output
Audience-appropriate copy that is warm for individuals, efficiency-focused for businesses, and clear/direct for developers.
